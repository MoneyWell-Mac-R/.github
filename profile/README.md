## How Bucket Budgeting Works

The defining feature of moneywell mac is its buckets. You split incoming cash into envelopes, each transaction
draws down the bucket it belongs to, and a bucket that's running low is obvious long before you overspend. When
priorities change, you roll money between buckets without breaking the plan. That's what makes budgeting app mac
genuinely proactive rather than a monthly autopsy of your bank balance.
